About

We think that it is exciting to look into the future and develop the technical solutions of tomorrow everyday.

With great competence and quality, we offer our customers special expertise. We work for regulated industries, such as medical and automotive where the requirements on reliability, stability, performance and system integration know-how are extremely high.

The company provides thorough engineering and research support, including design and professional support in the various aspects of mechanical engineering.

These skills and our unique specialty are offered to the company's clients as part of a comprehensive package that begins with development, and continues all the way through to the final stage of manufacturing.

The company makes it our mission to create solutions that fit the individual needs of each client and ensure their satisfaction.
